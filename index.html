Square Shopify integration connects your Square POS system with your Shopify online store. With this integration, product information, inventory levels, and order details automatically sync between both platforms.

Instead of updating products manually on each platform, businesses can manage everything from one place. When a product sells in your Shopify store, the inventory is automatically updated in Square. Similarly, when you make changes in Square, those updates reflect in Shopify.
